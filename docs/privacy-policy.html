Privacy Policy for Android Epson Reset

Last updated: March 12, 2026

Android Epson Reset is an unofficial utility for compatible Epson printers connected through USB OTG. This app is designed to run locally on your Android device and communicate directly with the connected printer.

Information We Collect

Android Epson Reset does not collect, sell, or share personal data for analytics, advertising, or profiling.

The app may temporarily access the following information only for its core functionality:

  • USB device information required to detect and connect to a compatible Epson printer
  • Printer information returned by the connected device, such as model name, device identifier, status, ink levels, and waste counter values
  • Protocol logs generated locally on your device for debugging and troubleshooting

How Information Is Used

The information accessed by the app is used only to:

  • Detect and connect to a supported USB printer
  • Read printer status and maintenance information
  • Send printer maintenance commands requested by the user
  • Display protocol and diagnostic information inside the app

Data Sharing

Android Epson Reset does not transmit your data to Tekware or to third-party servers. Printer communication takes place directly between your Android device and the connected printer over USB.

Data Storage

The app does not require user account registration and does not maintain a remote user database. Diagnostic information shown in the app is intended for local use only.

Permissions

The app requests USB permission from Android only when needed to communicate with a connected printer. This permission is used exclusively for printer access.

Third-Party Links

The app may include links to the project repository or related resources. If you open an external link, you will leave the app and be subject to the privacy practices of the destination site.

Children's Privacy

This app is not directed to children and does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

This policy may be updated from time to time. Any updated version will be published on this page.
